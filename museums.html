Museums

Another museum which is worth seeing is the Hadji Nikoli Inn Museum which is situated inside the 19th century home of wealthy local tradesman, Hadji Nikoli. It is a beautiful example of the Bulgarian Revival architecture and the only surviving inn from that period.

The Archaeological Museum is worth seeing while being in the city. It covers the region’s history from prehistoric times through the Middle Ages. The thousandyear-old history and culture of the region of Tarnovo is traced out there. The accent is on the period of the Second Bulgarian Kingdom.
