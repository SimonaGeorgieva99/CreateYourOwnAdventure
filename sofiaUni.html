Sofia University

The Sofia University St. Kliment Ohridski was the first higher educational institution in Bulgaria. Its history is a continuation of centuries of cultural and educational tradition in the country. Its prestige grew rapidly and it has become an important academic and scientific centre on the Balkans. There are two people sitting in front of the building. They are the two Bulgarian entrepreneurs: Evlogi Georgiev and his brother Hristo Georgiev

Sofia’s most famous and olderst park was landscaped after the Bulgarian Revival and its name is Borisova garden. There are a lot of places where kids can play, in the summer evenings there are free concerts to catch but you can also enjoy one sunny evening on the benches around the trees and the green area. There are some kiosks selling drinks and ice cream and restaurant serving fish and other traditional Bulgarian food.
